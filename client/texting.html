Purvey

Purvey is a food-tech organisation that provides easy access to food for low income earners by connecting them to donors that are ready to support them and help them thrive.

OUR SOLUTION

Over 80 million people globally are living below the poverty line. Many of these people cannot afford basic necessities like food. We are creating opportunities for easy and equal access to high quality and affordable food for all!

Purvey is built on a blockchain platform to deliver value on a platform of trust.

COVID-19 FOODBANK

The COVID-19 Food drive is providing food for the needy and vulnerable in the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic. Our partner on this project is the Green Investment Network

MISSION

Our mission is to connect 5 million undernourished Africans to a consistent supply of good quality and affordable food by 2025.

VISION

To be a leading technology-driven social enterprise providing sustainable and scalable solutions to the global hunger crisis.
